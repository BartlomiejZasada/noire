Why Synapsis?

Synapsis is not just a trading bot – it is a dual-purpose AI system designed to operate autonomously on financial markets while also capable of conducting advanced historical and geospatial analysis. Its agent-based architecture enables real-time learning, feedback loops, and independent decision-making.

Backtested at +160% annual performance (Sharpe >2), Synapsis is the financial engine and analytical brain of the NOIRE operation.

The NOIRE Mission

Somewhere in the world, the greatest treasure in automotive history lies hidden: the Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic – La Voiture Noire. Our goal is to locate it using AI, historical data, community intelligence, and obsessive research.

This is not a marketing stunt. This is a structured, AI-supported investigation supported by real capital and global collaboration.
