The Investigation

Somewhere in the world, the greatest treasure in automotive history is waiting to be found. A machine of impossible beauty, speed and mystery. A ghost on wheels. The Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic – La Voiture Noire – chassis 57453.

Driven by Jean Bugatti himself. Lost during World War II. Never recovered.

But what if we could find it?

NOIRE is not just a token. It’s the ignition key to a global investigation. A community-fueled movement powered by blockchain, driven by obsession, guided by data — and elevated by belief. We are building the world’s first decentralized intelligence network for historical investigation.
