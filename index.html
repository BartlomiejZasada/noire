The Investigation

Somewhere in the world, the greatest treasure in automotive history is waiting to be found. A machine of impossible beauty, speed and mystery. A ghost on wheels. The Bugatti Type 57SC Atlantic – La Voiture Noire – chassis 57453.

But what if we could find it?
